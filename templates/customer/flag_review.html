Community Guidelines

Help us maintain a safe and respectful community by flagging reviews that violate our guidelines. Our moderation team will review all flagged content within 24-48 hours.

Flag for:

  • Hate speech or discrimination
  • Spam or fake reviews
  • Personal information sharing
  • Off-topic or irrelevant content

Don't flag for:

  • Disagreement with opinion
  • Negative but constructive feedback
  • Personal preference differences
  • Restaurant owner disputes

What happens next?

  1. 1. Your flag will be reviewed by our moderation team
  2. 2. The review may be temporarily hidden during review
  3. 3. Appropriate action will be taken based on our guidelines
  4. 4. You'll be notified of the decision via email

Note: False or malicious flagging may result in account restrictions. Please only flag content that genuinely violates our community guidelines.
